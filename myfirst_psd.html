Q Homes (Handy Man) is Qatar’s first and only company dedicated to servicing villa and apartment maintenance requests

Whether it’s to repair a plumbing leak, clean out air conditioning (AC) ducts or install light fixtures, Q Homes (Handy Man) is here to help. Our goal is to service the needs of Western and professional expatriate tenants in Qatar with high quality and on-par with what you experienced back home:

  • E-maintenance ticket submission direct from our website accessible to you 24/7 wherever you have an Internet connection

  • 4-hour callback response guarantee for requests received during business hours, or next-day callback response guarantee for requests received after business hours

  • Professionally-trained customer service staff to help you through your repair or install/upgrade needs

  • Clear, simple and transparent pricing structure so you know how much you will pay and what you get for it

  • Professionally-trained maintenance team with uniforms and IDs and a maintenance supervisor who is fully fluent in the English language

Q Homes (Handy Man) is part of the Q Homes family of companies dedicated to raising the bar for customer service in the Qatari market.
